Fun Dog fact you should know

Dogs have a powerful sense of smell and can detect scents that are too subtle for humans to detect. They have up to 300 million olfactory receptors in their noses, compared to only 6 million in humans.

Dogs are highly social animals and can become depressed if left alone for long periods without social interaction. They thrive on attention and affection from their human family members.

Dogs are capable of hearing sounds that are too high for humans to hear. They can also locate the source of a sound more easily than humans can, making them excellent watchdogs.
